About Me

I'm a results-oriented Product Owner with 17+ years of experience in IT and over 5 years leading product strategy for SaaS and PaaS platforms. I specialize in aligning business goals with user-centered product experiences. My strength lies in combining Agile practices with data-driven insights to prioritize effectively, deliver fast, and build solutions that create measurable value. Known for my quick learning, strong ownership, and collaborative approach, I enjoy working across global teams to turn ideas into scalable, real-world products.

IoT Platform
